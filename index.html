The Consistency Advantage

Regular posting is a powerful signal to the algorithm. Consistent creators can receive up to 5x more engagement per post. This signals that your account is active and valuable, encouraging wider distribution.

Community is King

Move beyond broadcasting. Actively respond to comments and DMs, use interactive stickers, and collaborate with other creators. Building a community signals creator credibility and strengthens relationship history, both key ranking factors.
